# ReceAI Design Context

## Target Audience
- Small to medium-sized transportation businesses (limo companies, chauffeur services, tour operators)
- Business owners and managers in the transportation industry
- Tech-savvy but busy professionals who need efficient tools

## Use Cases
- Generate professional invoices and receipts quickly
- Manage business finances and accounting
- Track expenses and income for tax purposes
- Create branded documents for clients
- Streamline administrative workflows

## Brand Personality
- **Purpose**: Professional, reliable, efficient business tool for transportation industry
- **Tone**: Clean, modern, trustworthy, slightly premium
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Professional minimalism with subtle tech sophistication
- **Differentiation**: AI-powered precision in document generation for transportation businesses

## Design Constraints
- Next.js 14 + TailwindCSS framework
- Must be accessible and mobile-responsive
- Performance-optimized for business users
- Clean, professional appearance suitable for B2B context
